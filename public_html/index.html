Q: What's the longest hot air balloon flight?

A: On January 15, 1991, the Virgin Pacific Flyer balloon completed the longest flight in a hot air balloon when Per Lindstrand and Richard Branson flew 7,671.91 km (4,767.10 mi) from Japan to Northern Canada.

Q: What do you have to do to be a pilot?

A: In the United States, a pilot of a hot air balloon must have a pilot certificate from the Federal Aviation Administration and it must carry the rating of "Lighter-than-air free balloon."
